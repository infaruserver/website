The Edict of Anguielle


JEAN-JACQUES the Fourty-Second, By the GRACE of the SUN, King of Aréole, and Sovereign of all of her Colonies, has dictated this EDICT to the Scribes of the «SOLAR ORDER» - Whereas the disgraced Réjan-Soleil Gaillard failed to establish a colony in the remote islandes of Anguille, We hereby proclaim the Kingdom’s intent to claim the lost lands around where the ROYAL TRADING COMPANY established a post. Any ADVENTURER, EXPLORER, SAILOR, SETTLER and the such who is in allegiance to Us is therefore permitted to claim the lands there for Our realm under the known Authority of Our Crown.

THUS, We issue a PRIME of ONE DINAR for every islande larger than five hundred and twelve square meters charted, claimed and named for Our Realm.

FURTHERMORE, by the end of the month of Prairial [10 Feb 2024] of this fourth year of Our reign We fully intend on gifting to the governor of, by the GRACE and LIGHT of the SUN, the most bountiful and successful SETTLEMENT, a Charter of VICEROYALTY over all the COLONIES and SETTLEMENTS of the islandes.

By Writ of Royal Seal,
JEAN-JACQUES XLII
