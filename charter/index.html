Charter of the Territory of Anguille


JEAN-JACQUES the Fourty-ſecond, By the GRACE of the SUN, King of Aréole, and ſovereign of all of her Colonies, has dictated this CHARTER to the ſcribes of the «SOLAR ORDER» - Whereas We recieved a petition by many diverſe Noblemen, Adventurers, Merchants and the ſuch to lay claim upon the Islandes that were discovered by the Explorer ſieur Réjan-Soleil Gaillard, who already has been granted his Governorſhip of the Colony of Marinade which he diſcovered in the very ſame expedition - And thus We vouchſafe unto them the CROWN’s Licenſe to make Habitation, Plantation, and to erect a Colony of our People in thoſe Islands of the New World henceforth known as ANGUILLE after Our beloved animal friend the Eel which lives in the Academy of Oceanic ſtudies of BREST, and other eſtates and countries in the New World not now poſseſsed by Solist Peoples.

HENCEFORTH We do ordain, decree, and conſent, for Us, our Heirs, and ſucceſsors; that ſaid Colony ſhall be appointed a GOVERNOR, who ſhall govern and command all matters Diplomatic and Bellicus that ſhall arise within the Colony, and to enſure and conſerve fidelity for Crown and Country amongſt our loving and ſundry ſubjects, according to ſuch Laws and Ordinances that ſhall be in that Behalf, given and signed under the Royal ſeal of the Realm of Aréole.

And We do FURTHERMORE, for Us, Our Heirs, and ſucceſsors, by these Letters, CONFER and CONSENT; in order to adminiſtrate the Colonies, an INTENDENT, Each of which appointed and removed from time to time according as ſhall be inſtructed by means of ELECTION by the Free Landowners within the Colony, who can be known to be reſponſible and ſteward land, as per the inſtructions hereunto aſcribed. We also CONFIRM and AGREE unto the INTENDENT the ſuperior intendency and adminiſtration only of and for All matters that pertain or ſhall pertain to Government, who every month ſhall be re-elected by all who have purchaſed land within the Colony. They are reſponſible for hearing the petitions of the Colony and tranſmitting them to the Governor, and authoring decrees of law, which all ſhall be approved by ſaid Governor under Authority of the Royal ſeal of the Realm of Aréole.

And LASTLY, We command for Us, our Heirs and Succeſsors, grant to said Governor and Intendent, and their Succeſsors, by theſe Preſents, That theſe our Letters Patents ſhall be firm, good, and effectual in the Law, to all intents and Purpoſes according to Our true intent herein before Declared and the Certainty of our Premiſes, in the Aréolean Colony of Anguille, in the Occidental aforeſaid. in Witneſs thereof, We have cauſed theſe our Letters to be done Patent; Witneſs Ourſelf at Brest, the Three and Twentieth Day of Floréal, in the Fourth Year of Our Reign.

By Writ of Royal Seal,
JEAN-JACQUES XLII
