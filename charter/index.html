The Charter of the Territories of Anguille


JEAN-JACQUES the Fourty-Second, By the GRACE of the SUN, King of Aréole, and Sovereign of all of her Colonies, has dictated this CHARTER to the Scribes of the «SOLAR ORDER» - As the EDICT OF ANGUILLE dictated, We promised to grant a gift of VICEROYALTY to the head of the most successful and bountiful of Settlements - And as many of the residents of the COLONIES in Anguille have written to Us thankfully We were able to make the swiftest of decisions. We, King of Aréole, and Sovereign of all of her Colonies bestow to SMITH JEFFERSON the title of VICEROY OF ANGUILLE.

HENCEFORTH - We do ordain, decree, and consent, for Us, our Heirs and Successors that the Viceroy of Anguille shall REPRESENT Our holy and luminous CROWN and GOVERN all matters Diplomatic, Bellicus and of Administration that shall arise within the Colonies, and to ensure and conserve fidelity for Crown and Country amongst our loving and sundry subjects, according to such Laws and Ordinances that shall be in that Behalf, given and signed under the Royal seal of Aréole.

And LASTLY, We command for Us, our Heirs and Successors, grant to said Viceroy, and their Successors, by these Presents, That these our Letters Patents shall be firm, good, and effectual in the Law, to all intents and Purposes according to Our true intent herein before Declared and the Certainty of our Premises, in the Aréolean Colony of Anguille, in the Occidental aforesaid. in Witness thereof, We have caused these our Letters to be done Patent; Witness Ourself at Brest, the Twenty First Day of Pluviose, in the Fourth Year of Our Reign.

By Writ of Royal Seal,
JEAN-JACQUES XLII
