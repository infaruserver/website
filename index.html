- What is Infaru? -

FORGE A NATION'S DESTINY

The colony of Infaru has just gained its independence from her motherland, but now she has no one to govern her lands! Join your fellow players in establishing towns, forming a government, and uniting the lands of Infaru under this newly-independent nation!

JOIN A COMPLEX POLITICAL LANDSCAPE

With any new government comes new laws, bills, and policies to govern the land! With the power in your hands, you can create and pass laws or vote and protest against them: the choice is yours!

RUN YOUR OWN TOWN OR JOIN ONE

Kroletfaru was Infaru's former capital and centre of government, but players aren't bound by their origins! Venture off into a large 7.5k by 7.5k map and establish your own town, make new laws, and govern how YOU want to govern!

TREAD WITH CAUTION

The reach of law isn't widespread, so be careful the further you venture out. Whenever you leave the borders of Infaru, you enter a no-man's land. Any territory outside of Infaru is not protected by the government, which means anything is allowed outside of her borders.

A WELCOMING COMMUNITY

With a diverse staff team and player base, Infaru is a welcoming and inclusive server for all races, genders, sexualities, and identities alike. All of us will respect you as long as you respect others!

- Connecting -

Infaru is a Java Vanilla server with no mods required to join. Infaru runs Geyser, which allows Bedrock clients to connect and play.


If you are on Java, you can use the optional mod Simple Voicechat to enable voicechat. The mod can be downloaded here.

Connecting with Java

Use version 1.21 or above and connect to play.infaru.gay

Connecting with Bedrock

Connect to the IP address play.infaru.gay with port 25565 in your external server settings.
