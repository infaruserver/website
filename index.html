- What is Infaru? -

A NEW LAND

We have left our home of Norfaru behind. We have dared to go where none have treaded before to create something new: Vinfaru. Build a new country from the ground up with your fellow players and simulate society together. The possibilities are limitless!

A ROLEPLAY EXPERIENCE

This isn't just about the big picture. Create a character and play out your own story in a character-oriented roleplay experience. In Infaru, characters aren't just you; they have traits, motives, and beliefs that they abide to. Lead your Seraph through the hardships and joys of life out in the cold frontier of Vinfaru.

A PLAYER DRIVEN SANDBOX

There is no "end-goal" to Infaru. There is simply the simulation of what a long term civilization might look like in the world of Vintage Story. The story is completely player-driven. The plot is what you make of it, and the stories are what you find in others, not what game masters want you to do.

A WELCOMING COMMUNITY

With a diverse staff team and player base, Infaru is a welcoming and inclusive server for all races, genders, sexualities, and identities alike. All of us will respect you as long as you respect others!
